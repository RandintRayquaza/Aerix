# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Creators, influencers, writers, designers, and developers who need a professional, unified public profile to share their links, media, and branding, and to analyze audience engagement.

## Product Purpose
A creator identity platform that allows creators to build custom public profile pages and track their performance through dashboard analytics. It serves to elevate creator content through outstanding design.

## Brand Personality
Premium, editorial, sophisticated. Focuses on classic typography, rich contrast, restrained layouts, and high-caliber motion that highlights rather than hides the product content.

## Anti-references
- The generic SaaS template: tiny uppercase kicked eyebrows on every section, over-rounded ghost cards (border + huge shadow), and text gradient overlays.
- Hyper-saturated or chaotic crypto/Web3 interfaces that prioritize flashiness over readability and utility.
- Rigid, boxy enterprise dashboards that lack character or fail to feel premium.

## Design Principles
1. **Editorial Restraint**: Elevate the creator's content. Use generous whitespace, high-contrast typography, and restrained decoration so the layout feels like a high-end magazine rather than a busy application.
2. **Precision Utility**: The builder and dashboard interfaces must feel responsive, predictable, and fast. Form follows function with clear visual hierarchies and readable data visualizations.
3. **High-Contrast Clarity**: Ensure strong readability at all times. Never use low-contrast body text. Typography size, line-height, and weight should create an effortless hierarchy.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Achieve WCAG 2.1 AA contrast levels (minimum 4.5:1 for body copy).
- Provide clear, accessible focus states (`:focus-visible`) for all interactive elements.
- Honor user motion preferences via `@media (prefers-reduced-motion: reduce)`.
